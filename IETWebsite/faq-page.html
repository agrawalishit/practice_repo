FAQs:

What is the last date to apply for admission?

The last date for submitting the admission form is 31st July 2026.

What documents are required during admission?

You need your Aadhaar Card, 10th Marksheet, 12th Marksheet, passport-size photographs, and transfer certificate.

Is hostel accommodation available for first-year students?

Yes, hostel facilities are available for both boys and girls on a first-come, first-served basis.

Can I apply for more than one course?

Yes, you can select multiple course preferences during the application process.

How will I know if my admission is confirmed?

You will receive a confirmation email and SMS after successful document verification and fee payment.
